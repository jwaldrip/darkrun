Decide a darkrun Checkpoint.

Review the holding Station via `/darkrun:darkrun-show`, then call `darkrun_checkpoint_decide` (`approve` to lock and advance, or `request_changes` with `notes` to route rework as drift) and continue with `/darkrun:darkrun-pickup`. See the `darkrun-checkpoint` skill.
