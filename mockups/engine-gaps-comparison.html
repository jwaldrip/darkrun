What we ported, and what we missed

Every row read in the predecessor engine (~85K LOC) and verified against darkrun's Rust source. Each darkrun cell carries a verdict pill and a file:line citation. 44 mechanisms checked: 3 present, 15 partial, 26 missing.

The truth, after verification. darkrun ported the predecessor's shape (three tracks, phase machine, checkpoints, iterations, stamps, surface proof) and — correcting the first draft — more of its safety layer than I credited: a full 8-hook agent guard suite, a cursor-level deadlock/churn halt, per-path drift idempotency, and a drift-witness log. What's still genuinely absent is the witness-based drift immune system (premise/input drift, restamp-on-detect, verifier nonce, integrity checksum, dispatch-lease recovery, fix-chain isolation), the quality-gate execution engine, and — the cluster that started this — records that carry the story (an iteration has no "why", feedback no origin or closure reply).

what this verified picture says

darkrun is further along on safety than the first draft implied — the hook guard suite, agent-write stamping, deadlock/churn halt, and per-path drift idempotency are all real and load-bearing.

The genuine debt clusters into three things. (1) Cluster A — records hold verdicts, not stories; cheap to close and the highest daily value. (2) The witness-premise half of cluster B — input drift, restamp-on-detect, verifier nonce, integrity checksum, dispatch-lease recovery, fix-chain isolation — the parts that keep a long autonomous run from silently corrupting. (3) Cluster D — quality gates are declared in content but the engine never runs them, so a "passed Audit" today isn't backed by executed tests.

Suggested order still holds: A → witness/recovery half of B → D → C → E/F/G.

Every remaining line now has an explicit disposition — CLOSED (committed), DELIBERATE (darkrun chose a different design on purpose), or DEFERRED (scoped, with why-not-yet) — in the companion engine-parity-gaps.md. Highlights: the big deferred items are D1 (quality-gate execution), B9 (fix-chain worktree isolation), and C1 (multi-signal elaborate loop); the deliberate non-gaps are B2 (revert-self-heal beats auto-restamp), B6 (hooks cover hookless tamper), and C2 (the fixed six stations are the point).
